## Minor 1 — Hyperparameter Analysis of Linear SVM

Analysed the effect of `C`, `max_iter`, and `tol` on a **LinearSVC** classifier trained on synthetic 2D data generated by `genChallengeData(level, n)`.

- Proved mathematically that the dataset is **strictly linearly separable** for all valid `level` and `n`.
- Small challenge level (`level=0.3`): perfect accuracy with `C ≈ 0.0047`, `max_iter=1`, `tol ≈ 2.99`.
- Large challenge level (`level=51`): requires `C ≈ 886.88`, `max_iter=25`, `tol ≤ 2×10⁻⁴`.
- Training time grows as **O(N·d·T)** with dataset size; accuracy improves gradually and saturates.

## Minor 2 — Generative Classifier with Missing Pixels

Implemented a **Gaussian generative classifier** on MNIST with censored (zeroed) central pixels, deriving a single-step joint inference procedure for simultaneous classification and reconstruction.

- Single-step score adds an extra `−½ log|Σ̄_c|` penalty over the two-step lecture method.
- Used `numpy.linalg.solve` + `slogdet` + diagonal regularisation (ε=10⁻⁶) for numerical stability.
- Single-step slightly outperforms at low censoring (≤8%) but degrades at high censoring (51%) due to ill-conditioned conditional covariance.
- Best accuracy: **80.31%** (single-step) vs **80.25%** (two-step) at 2% missing pixels.

## Minor 3 — EM Mixture-of-Experts for AQI Regression

Applied an **EM-based mixture of Ridge regression experts** to predict ground-truth O₃ concentration (ppb) from the AQI-SATVAM sensor dataset (10,458 points, October 2019, IIT Kanpur).

- Optimal configuration: **K=4 components**, **n_iter=30** → Test MAE = **9.43 ppb**.
- Components naturally discover the diurnal O₃ cycle (low/medium/high ozone regimes by time-of-day and temperature).
- Most important features: `o3op1(mV)` (ΔMAE=+4.24) and `Temp(C)` (ΔMAE=+4.22).
- Outperforms best Decision Tree (depth=10, MAE=10.34 ppb) by **0.92 ppb**, at the cost of being 5.66× slower at inference.
